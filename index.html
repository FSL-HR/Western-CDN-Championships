Female Super League · Playoffs

How it works. Scores save automatically to this browser — reopen the file anytime and your results are still here. In best-of-3 series, the first team to 2 wins advances automatically and drops into the next round. Region champions flow into the Western Canadian Championship round robin; standings and medal-round matchups fill themselves in from the games you enter. Use Save to download a JSON file of everything — every score, seed, ice time, and scheduled matchup — and Undo to step back through your recent changes. To schedule games, click Time template to download a CSV of every game (with a fixed game_id for each), fill in the date, time, and rink columns, then click Import ice times to load them. Times attach to the correct game slot automatically, even before the teams are decided.
